Instructions

In this task, you will rate the similarity of relationships between words. On each page, you will first see a pair of words, such as:


kitten : cat


You will be asked to think of the relationship between these words (for example, a kitten is a young cat). Then, after a brief delay, you will see a different pair of words, such as:


chick : chicken


Your task is to rate how similar the relationship between the second pair of words is to the relationship between the first pair of words. In this example, the second relationship (a chick is a young chicken) is very similar to the first one, even though the two pairs of words are about different kinds of animals.
